Privacy Policy

GrassCutterGame.com is built for quick, free play. This policy explains what information may reach us when you visit, why it may be used, and the choices available to you.

Effective July 23, 2026

01

Information We Collect

You can play on GrassCutterGame.com without creating an account or submitting your name, address, or payment details. We do not ask for sensitive personal information to provide the game.

Like most websites, our hosting systems may receive basic technical data when your browser requests a page. This can include an IP address, browser and device type, referring page, requested URL, and the date and time of the request. If you email us, we receive the email address and information you choose to include in your message.

02

How We Use Information

We use limited technical information only for practical website operations, such as delivering pages, keeping the service secure, diagnosing errors, understanding broad site performance, and preventing misuse. Information sent by email is used to answer the request, investigate a reported problem, or improve the site.

We do not sell personal information, build advertising profiles from your gameplay, or require personal data in exchange for access to the game.

03

Third-Party Services

We rely on service providers for essential infrastructure such as hosting, content delivery, security, and email. Those providers may process technical request data on our behalf under their own terms and privacy practices. We choose services that support reliable delivery and limit our use of data to what is reasonably needed to operate the website.

Links that take you to another website are governed by that website's privacy policy. We encourage you to review it before sharing information there.

04

Cookies

GrassCutterGame.com does not require an account cookie to play. We do not use cookies to create a cross-site advertising profile. Essential storage or similar browser technology may be used by our infrastructure when needed for security, traffic management, or reliable page delivery.

You can control cookies and site storage through your browser settings. Blocking essential technology may affect how some website features work, but the core game is designed to remain simple and accessible.

05

Children's Privacy

The game is available to a broad audience, but the website is not designed to collect personal information from children. We do not knowingly request or collect personal information from anyone under 13. Children should not send names, contact details, or other personal information through email or any future interactive feature without a parent or guardian's permission.

If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can review the request and remove the information where appropriate.

06

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or a privacy request can be sent to support@grasscuttergame.com. Please describe the page or issue involved so we can respond effectively.
