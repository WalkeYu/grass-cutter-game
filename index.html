02

Quick answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Grass Cutter?

Draw one blade with a mouse or finger, review its span, balance, and flow, then start the automatic race. Your mower follows the course by itself, so the blade you create determines how efficiently the grass is cut.

Is Grass Cutter free to play?

Yes. Grass Cutter is free to play in the game frame on this page, and you do not need to install anything before starting.

Can I play Grass Cutter on mobile?

Yes. The page adapts to phones and tablets, and you can draw the blade directly with one finger. After you start the race, both mowers move automatically.

What are the controls for Grass Cutter?

On desktop, hold the primary mouse button and drag inside the blade workshop. On a touchscreen, press and slide one finger, then release. There are no movement controls during the race.

What is the objective of Grass Cutter?

Create a wide, balanced, clean blade that clears more grass than the rival blade. Better designs improve cutting reach and efficiency, helping you earn more food for the pets.
